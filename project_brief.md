# Project Brief

## Name

Dream Queue AI - queue and revenue management system for amusement parks.

## One-liner

We help amusement parks reduce queues and earn more through virtual slots, load forecasting, guest routing, and dynamic fast-pass inventory.

## Case Study

Dream Island, Moscow.

## Positioning

The MVP is not a replacement for park infrastructure. It is an analytical layer and simulation prototype that shows the value of controlled queue inventory, demand redistribution, and revenue optimization.

## Core MVP Questions

- How often do guests complain about queues and crowding?
- Which attractions are likely to create bottlenecks?
- How much revenue can paid fast slots create?
- Can route recommendations reduce waiting time without increasing physical capacity?
- What operator dashboard would make this useful for park managers?

## Pitch Angle

The park loses money when people stand in queues instead of buying food, merchandise, games, and extra entertainment. Dream Queue AI turns waiting into a managed and monetizable guest flow.

## Near-Term Pitch Direction

Use Genting SkyWorlds + Alibaba Cloud as a category benchmark: a major theme park already adopted AI-powered Virtual Queue, itinerary planning, crowd prediction, and incentives.

For Dream Island, the first offer should be lighter:

- analyze current public evidence and pain points;
- model likely bottlenecks;
- simulate baseline vs optimized guest flow;
- estimate fast-slot revenue scenarios;
- show an operator dashboard prototype;
- propose a low-risk 4-8 week pilot before deep integration.
