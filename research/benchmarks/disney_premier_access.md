# Disney Premier Access Benchmark

## Pattern

Paid priority access can be sold at attraction level or bundled by park/day depending on market.

## Lesson for Dream Queue AI

Single-attraction fast slots are easier to test as a mass-market layer than expensive full-day VIP access.

## Product Difference

Dream Queue AI combines paid inventory with free virtual queues and demand redistribution. The goal is not only upsell, but also smoother park operations.
